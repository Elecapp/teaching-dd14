Diving into the web

Exploring official data Back to main page Visualization for advocacy

In the Final Synthesis Design Studio, students learn to use information visualization to observe, understand, shape and communicate complex phenomena.

In the second part of the course, students map a controversial issue by collecting and analyzing online data. Using Digital Methods, students locate a controversy on the web, identify actors, and map their positions and alignments. The result is a visual report that presents the issue under study, its main actors, and their positions.
